My Journey

As a Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) with 5+ years in the fast-paced clinical and healthcare domain, I've built my career on one principle: translating complex strategy into real-world value. At Clario, I evolved from a Clinical BA to a Senior BA, where I led strategic initiatives, mentored teams, and delivered results. My world was built on Agile Methodology, Data-Driven Decisions, and meticulous Stakeholder Management. I didn't just manage requirements; I optimized the entire product lifecycle, delivering 50% of projects ahead of schedule by refining product backlogs and reducing communication overhead by 30%. I realized my passion wasn't just analyzing the "how," but defining the "why." This led me to earn a PG Degree in Data Science and my CSPO, and to found "The Data Cell" (my Substack) to explore AI and product strategy. I am not just a PO who understands technology; I am a data-driven builder who understands business, users, and the path to market.
